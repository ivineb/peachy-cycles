Bicycles are essential vehicles for transportation justice. Riding a bicycle means freedom, peace, adventure, joy, and happiness. A bicycle is a source of inspiration for going out, pushing yourself, meeting others, and exploring your surroundings.

Similar to most industries, the cycling scene in Minneapolis has a gender and racial gap, bike rides are mostly done by white men.


This project centers around creating a platform that brings inclusivity to the cycling industry. It empowers and provides connection, support and resources to the underserved communities. Peachy Cycles is a safe space for women, BIPOC, and LGBTQ+ who love to bike.
